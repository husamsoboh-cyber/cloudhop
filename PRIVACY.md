# Privacy Policy

**CloudHop collects zero data.**

- CloudHop runs entirely on your computer
- No analytics, no telemetry, no tracking
- Your files transfer directly between cloud providers
- No data passes through any CloudHop server
- No account required to use CloudHop
- Transfer logs are stored locally on your machine at `~/.cloudhop/`

CloudHop is open source. You can verify this by reading the code.
